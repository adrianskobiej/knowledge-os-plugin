# Content strategy

Content has one goal: deliver qualified leads into the [[sales-process]].

## Channels

- Blog — expert articles, SEO.
- Newsletter — every two weeks, owned list.
- Social — distribution and reach building.

## The 80/20 rule

80% educational content, 20% sales content.
