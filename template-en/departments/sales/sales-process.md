# Sales process

The standard B2B funnel at the company. Process owner: [[anna-smith]].

## Stages

1. Lead — acquiring the contact (marketing → see [[content-strategy]]).
2. Qualification — does the client fit the profile and have a budget.
3. Offer — preparing and presenting the proposal.
4. Negotiation — agreeing on terms.
5. Close — signing and handing off to onboarding.

## Rules

- Every lead lands in the CRM within 24h.
- The offer is always in writing, with an expiry date.
